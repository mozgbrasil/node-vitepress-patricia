Mobile ecosystem ​

  • Google Play: the official developer profile concentrates Android distribution and makes the mobile surface publicly traceable.
  • Mozg TWA: brings the main site identity into the Android experience.
  • Mozg Híbrido: expands the mobile presence with a second published surface.
  • Auditable distribution: website, apps and public artifacts keep clear policies, verifiable links and coordinated updates.

Public artifacts and traceability ​

  • Main website: mozg.com.br concentrates dossiers, portfolio, stacks, APIs and the central technical narrative.
  • Complementary portal: mozgbrasil.github.io reinforces identity, portfolio and complementary public distribution.
  • Public design system: the node-web-components project dossier connects the technical dossier to the published Storybook and the npm package.
  • Civic and public trust surfaces: Brasil Participativo, Trustpilot and the privacy policy strengthen public traceability beyond the code workspace.
